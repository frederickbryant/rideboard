A New Ridesharing App

A cheaper and faster alternative to Uber, Lyft, and public transit.

Before computers most colleges had something called a ride board. It gave everyone an opportunity to save money and maybe even make a new friend. There were two sections on the board. One for drivers and one for riders. People would put when and where they were traveling to and a way to get in contact with them. If someone saw a person they wanted to ride with they contact the other person and split the cost of gas.

With our app rideboard we are bringing in a modern take to this old idea. Lyft and Uber are great for short trips if you have enough people to split the cost, but for a longer rides there is no frugal solution. That is where we come in. With our app you will on average pay around 95% less than you would on a Lyft or Uber.
